Link funnel va hinh popup

Dat link 3s mac dinh, tuy chon 20s va 300s neu muon tach luong.
Day la link dich mac dinh cua popup 3s. Neu popup 20s va 300s de trong, chung cung se dung link nay.
Co the dan URL hoac chon anh tu may.
De trong neu muon dung lai anh popup 3s.
Co the de trong neu ban muon card comment gon, khong co anh lon.
